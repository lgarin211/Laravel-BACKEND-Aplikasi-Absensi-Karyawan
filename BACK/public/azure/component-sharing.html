Sharing

We've created an awesome system that allows our page to automatically grab and share the current link it's on. No need to manually add links. You can attatch it to absolutely any link, button or icon.

Tip: Use your Mobile

Please check this page on your mobile device in order for the sharing links to actually share the page you're on! These do not work in the demo iframe we have on ThemeForest.

Large Icons

Add social icons after any content block or element, after tapping it, the page will automatically generate the share link.
